About

Hello! I'm a second-year computer science PhD student at Harvard, advised by Milind Tambe. Previously, I completed my master's in computer science at Stanford, where I applied computer vision and natural language processing to solve problems in sustainability and education. My undergrad was a double major in CS and music (concentration in conducting), also at Stanford. During my PhD, I aim to combine my interests in decision-making under uncertainty and robustness with my passion for positive social impact.
