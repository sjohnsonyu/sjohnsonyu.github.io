About

Hello! I'm a senior studying computer science and music at Stanford. I love computer science for its empowering potential to create socially impactful solutions, and I'm passionate about music as a singer/conductor and also from a musicological perspective. When I'm not coding or practicing, you'll likely find me outdoors - trail running, bouldering, adventuring, or journaling.

While I see myself as a student in the world, an essential part of my learning is teaching. I teach CS sections, TA in the music department, and tutor in a variety of subjects, including music theory, algebra, and Latin. Helping others grow alongside me is my passion.

I'm an ideas person - I love the thrill of a working on a project, whether it's CS, music, organizing, or art. If you have any ideas that you want to bring to life with me, feel free to reach out!
