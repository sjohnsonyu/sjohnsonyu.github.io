About

Hello! I'm a junior studying computer science and music at Stanford, with interests in social justice and environmental activism. I study computer science because I see it as a tool for bridging worlds and creating socially impactful solutions. I study music because I live to experience it and can't live without it. If I'm not coding like crazy, discussing divestment, or singing my heart out in rehearsals, you'll likely find me outdoors - trail running, bouldering, adventuring, or working on outdoor art projects.

I'm a relatively young coder (having seen code for the first time in September 2015), so I bring a growth mindset, ready to learn something new every day. While I think of myself as an continual student of the world, an essential part of learning is teaching. I teach CS sections and tutor in a variety of subjects, including music theory, algebra, and Latin. Helping others grow alongside me is my passion.

There are few things more exciting to me in the world than the thrill of a new project, whether it's CS, music, organizing, or art. If you have any ideas that you want to bring to life, feel free to contact me!
