Projects

Data Arts Grantmaker Map

I'm currently working on a mapping tool for DataArts, an organization that connects grantmakers and cultural organizations. This tool provides grantmakers with a geospatial perspective of their applicants and offers a suite of ways to filter the data and better understand the makeup of the applicant pools.

Bay Area Music Map

I'm involved in the Bay Area Music Map Project, an effort sponsored by PeaceTones and Stanford's Center for Comparative Studies in Race and Ethnicity. We aim to create a tool that strengthens the system supporting the music community, providing a geospatial view of the music organization landscape and fulfilling the needs of stakeholders in the music community. This project is founded upon the values of community-engaged learning, so if you care about music in the Bay Area, please reach out! We'd love to hear your feedback on the project and incorporate your ideas into the design.
