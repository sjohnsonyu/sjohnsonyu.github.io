Photography

I started learning photography through a Bing Overseas Seminar on conservation photography in Costa Rica taught by Sue McConnell. I believe that an image can change the way a person sees the world and can transform how someone views themself. Since Costa Rica, I have focused more on honing my portrait skills. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in learning more about my work!
